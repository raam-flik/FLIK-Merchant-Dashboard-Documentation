# Cek Red Zone (Check Red Zone)

Check if an address is in a red zone or restricted delivery area. Red zones are areas where delivery is difficult, slow, or has additional charges.

## Overview

Cek Red Zone (Check Red Zone) allows you to:
- Check address delivery status
- Identify red zone areas
- Understand delivery restrictions
- View additional charges
- Plan shipping accordingly
- Manage customer expectations

## What is a Red Zone?

A **Red Zone** is an area where:
- Delivery is challenging
- Extended delivery time
- Additional shipping costs apply
- Limited carrier availability
- Remote/mountainous areas
- Island locations
- Congested urban areas
- Safety concerns

### Red Zone Impacts
- ✅ **Longer Delivery Time**: 5-10+ days vs. 1-3 days
- ✅ **Higher Costs**: Extra surcharge 20-50%+
- ✅ **Limited Carriers**: Fewer options available
- ✅ **Manual Processing**: May require special handling

## Red Zone Status Results

### Red Zone Confirmed (In Red Zone)

If address is in red zone:

#### Red Zone Information
- **Status**: RED ZONE
- **Zone Type**: Type of restriction
- **Severity**: Level 1-5 (1=minimal, 5=severe)
- **Color Code**: Visual indicator (Red)

#### Shipping Impact
- **Affected Carriers**: Which couriers don't deliver
- **Available Carriers**: Which couriers service this area
- **Additional Charges**: Surcharge amount
- **Estimated Delivery**: 5-10+ days typical
- **Special Requirements**: Any special handling

#### Additional Cost Breakdown
- **Base Rate**: Standard shipping cost
- **Red Zone Surcharge**: Extra amount
- **Total Cost**: Combined amount
- **Cost per carrier**: If multiple options

#### Alternatives
- **Express Options**: Premium delivery methods
- **Scheduled Delivery**: Arrange specific date
- **Partner Carriers**: Alternative couriers
- **Regional Service**: Local delivery options

### Green Zone (Not in Red Zone)

If address is clear:

#### Green Zone Information
- **Status**: GREEN ZONE / NORMAL
- **Zone Type**: Standard delivery area
- **Color Code**: Visual indicator (Green)

#### Shipping Benefits
- **All Carriers**: Full availability
- **Standard Rates**: No extra charges
- **Fast Delivery**: 1-3 days typical
- **Reliable Service**: High success rate
- **Multiple Options**: Full carrier choice

#### Normal Pricing
- **Carrier Rates**: Standard pricing
- **No Surcharge**: No additional fees
- **Competitive Pricing**: Best available rates
- **Service Reliability**: High service level

### Yellow Zone (Caution Area)

If address has potential issues:

#### Yellow Zone Information
- **Status**: CAUTION / BORDERLINE
- **Zone Type**: Potential issues exist
- **Color Code**: Visual indicator (Yellow)

#### Potential Concerns
- **May be Slow**: Possible delays
- **Possible Surcharge**: May apply extra charges
- **Limited Carriers**: Some carriers may not service
- **Verify Address**: May need confirmation

#### Recommendation
- Verify address with customer
- Provide extended delivery timeline
- Mention possible extra cost
- Get customer confirmation
