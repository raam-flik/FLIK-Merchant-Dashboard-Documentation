# General Settings

Access account and warehouse settings through the Gear Icon menu in the top navigation bar.

## Accessing Settings

Click the **Gear Icon** (⚙️) in the top navigation bar to open the settings menu.

![Gear Settings Clicked](../assets/screenshots/General/Gear%20Settings%20Clicked.png)

## Settings Menu Options

When you click the gear icon, a dropdown menu appears with two main options:

### 1. Pengaturan Akun (Account Settings)

**Purpose**: Manage your account/profile information

**What it includes**:
- Brand/Store Name
- Website URL
- Person In Charge (PIC) information:
  - Name
  - Phone Number
  - Email

**Access**: Click "Pengaturan Akun" from the gear menu

**Redirect**: Takes you to the [Profile Management](./profile.md) page

### 2. Warehouse (Warehouse Management)

**Purpose**: Manage warehouse/location information

**What it includes**:
- View list of all warehouses
- Add new warehouses
- Edit warehouse details
- Warehouse location and address information
- PIC (warehouse manager) details
- Warehouse hours and operational settings

**Access**: Click "Warehouse" from the gear menu

**Redirect**: Takes you to [Pengaturan Warehouse](./general-settings/warehouse.md) page

## Navigation Structure

```
Gear Icon Menu
├── Pengaturan Akun (Account Settings)
│   └── Profile Management Page
└── Warehouse (Warehouse Management)
    └── Warehouse Configuration Page
```

## Tips

- **Account Settings**: Update store branding and contact information
- **Warehouse**: Manage multiple warehouse locations and their details
- **Menu Location**: Gear icon is in the top-right area of every page
- **Quick Access**: Both options are easily accessible from any page
