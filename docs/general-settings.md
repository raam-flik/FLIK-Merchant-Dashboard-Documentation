# General Settings

Configure your FLIK merchant dashboard settings to customize your experience and manage business operations.

## Accessing Settings

### How to Open Settings
1. Click the **Gear Icon** (⚙️) in the top navigation bar
2. Select desired settings category
3. Make your changes
4. Click **Save** or **Simpan**

**Screenshot Reference**: `/assets/screenshots/General/Gear Settings Clicked.png`

## Settings Categories

### Store & Business Settings
Configure basic business information:
- **Store Name**: Display name of your store
- **Store Description**: Brief business description
- **Store Category**: Type of business
- **Store Address**: Physical location
- **Contact Number**: Primary business phone
- **Email Address**: Business email
- **Website**: Your website URL (optional)

## Advanced Settings

### Export Data
Export your store data:
1. Go to **Data & Backup**
2. Click **Export Data** or **Ekspor Data**
3. Select data types to export
4. Choose file format (CSV, Excel, JSON)
5. Download exported file

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Settings not saving | Check internet, refresh page, try again |
| Integration failing | Verify API keys, check permissions |
| Notifications not received | Check email settings, spam folder |
| Language not changing | Clear browser cache, refresh page |
| Backup not creating | Check available storage, try later |
