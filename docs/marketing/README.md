# Marketing Module

Manage customer relationships and marketing initiatives to grow your business.

## Overview

The Marketing module provides tools for customer management, lead tracking, and customer engagement.

## Features

### 1. Customer Management
Organize and manage customer information and relationships.
- View customer database
- Track purchase history
- Manage customer segments
- Customer analytics
- Communication history

[Read Customer Documentation →](./customer.md)

### 2. Leads Management
Track and manage potential customers and sales leads.
- Lead capture and organization
- Lead scoring
- Sales pipeline tracking
- Follow-up automation
- Conversion tracking

[Read Leads Management Documentation →](./leads-management.md)

## Quick Start

### Customer Management
1. View all customers
2. Search and filter customers
3. Track purchase behavior
4. Segment for marketing
5. Send targeted campaigns

### Leads Management
1. Capture new leads
2. Score and prioritize
3. Track interactions
4. Follow up automatically
5. Convert to customers

## Integration with Other Modules

- **Checkout**: Customer purchase data
- **Shipping**: Delivery addresses
- **Promotions**: Marketing campaigns
- **Analytics**: Performance metrics
