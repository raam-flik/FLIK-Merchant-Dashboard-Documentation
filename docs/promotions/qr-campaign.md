# QR Campaign

Create QR code-based marketing campaigns for easy promotion sharing and tracking.

## Overview

QR Campaign allows you to generate trackable QR codes linked to promotions.

## QR Campaign List Page

**Screenshot Reference**: `/assets/screenshots/Promosi - QR Campaign/List of QR Campaign.png`

View all QR campaigns with scan metrics and engagement data.

### Creating QR Campaign

**Screenshot Reference**: `/assets/screenshots/Promosi - QR Campaign/Create QR Campaign.png`

#### Campaign Details
- **Campaign Name**: Campaign identifier
- **Description**: Campaign description
- **Campaign Type**: Promotion type
- **Linked Promotion**: Which promotion/offer
- **Start Date**: Campaign start
- **End Date**: Campaign end

#### QR Configuration
- **Landing Page**: Where QR links to
- **Product Page**: Product or category
- **Voucher Code**: Auto-apply discount
- **Campaign Link**: Custom URL

#### Distribution
- **Print Materials**: For offline marketing
- **Digital Share**: Social media, email
- **Email Campaign**: Include in emails
- **SMS**: Share via text
- **Website**: Embed on site

### Manage Campaigns
- View scan statistics
- Track click-through rate
- Monitor conversions
- Share QR code
- Download or print
- Monitor engagement

## Tracking Metrics

- **QR Scans**: Total scans
- **Clicks**: Landing page clicks
- **Conversions**: From QR to purchase
- **Device Type**: Mobile vs desktop
- **Geographic Data**: Scan locations
- **Time Data**: When scans occur

## Best Practices

- Clear call to action
- Easy-to-reach landing page
- Mobile-optimized destination
- Track performance metrics
- Update QR codes regularly
- Test before distribution
- Multiple distribution channels
